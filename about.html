Why Fortune Cookies?

Because discovering the news is the best part of the cookie.

The Sweet Core of Communication

At Scriptie, we noticed an alarming trend in how the most important news is delivered: sterile emails, basic group chats, or cold corporate memorandums. Whether you're announcing a joyful pregnancy, sending out a dream job offer, or unfortunately letting someone go, the medium often lacks impact.

Why not give them something to chew on? Literally.

A fortune cookie has psychological impact. It demands attention. The snap of the shell is visceral. Pulling the strip of paper creates anticipation. By the time they read "Will you marry me?" or "Your department has been outsourced," the moment is already unforgettable.

A Legacy of Honesty

Scriptie was founded on one simple premise: Life is full of surprises, both good and bad. Instead of relying on predictable greeting cards or lifeless PDFs, we bake the truth into a crispy exterior. Deliver your best moments, or your worst updates, tucked perfectly into a piece of paper shaped like destiny.
